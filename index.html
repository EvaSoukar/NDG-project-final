About us

Our cozy vegetarian restaurant in NDG neighborhood, serves tasty and healthy Falafel and delicious oriental sweets. Our menu is a celebration of fresh, locally sourced ingredients, carefully selected to create dishes that are as delicious as they are nutritious.
And for those seeking keto-friendly options, stay tuned as we're soon introducing a selection of guilt-free sweets, perfect for indulging without compromising your dietary goals. Join us on a culinary journey where deliciousness meets healthfulness, and every meal is a celebration of good food made with love.
